VEGETABLE SALAD



A vegetable salad can be done in different ways - with raw materials,with boiled or steamed vegetable and also with grilled veggies.Whatever way you make it, a vegetable salad is absolutely great for your body as it increases the intake of your veggies

So here are some instructions and list of ingredients to prepare the delicious vegetable salad



  • LIST OF INGREDIENTS
    1. Vegetables
    2. Oil
    3. Vinegar
    4. Spices
    5. Seeds and nuts

    Directions:


    ->Wash all the vegetables throughly under running water.Wipe them dry.
    ->Optional-if using cabbage,shred it and add to hot boiling water,Leave for 2 minutes .Remove and add them to cold water and rinse well.Spread it in the colander and set aside to drain completely.
    ->Peel and chop the other vegetables to bite sized pieces.Grate bettroot and carrots.Add them to a large bowl.
    ->Dessed the green chillies or crush black pepper and set aside
    ->Add all the prepared vegetables,mint and coriander leaves to the bowl when you are ready to serve the salad, add salt , vinegar and black pepper.Toss them well.Lastly sprinkle oil and toss.
    ->Garnish with seeds or nuts.Serve vegetable salad immediately with any meal
